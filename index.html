Books

Welcome to my collection of free publications exploring the concepts of SuperProductivity and the future of human potential. These books examine how emerging technologies are reshaping our work, society, and civilization. Feel free to download them in either English or Italian.

SuperProductivity: The Next Frontier

My comprehensive exploration of how AI, automation, and emerging technologies will fundamentally transform human productivity in the 21st century. This book examines the technical, economic, and philosophical dimensions of the coming productivity revolution.

Published: 2025 | Pages: 80

Beyond Employment: Navigating the Age of Superproductivity

Explores how AI, quantum computing, and robotics are creating unprecedented productivity gains that may lead to post-scarcity scenarios. This book examines how the meaning of work will evolve when traditional employment is no longer necessary for survival, how organizations must restructure, and what skills will become more valuable in a highly automated world. It provides practical strategies for navigating this transition while ensuring technology serves human potential and well-being.

SmartSolver Concept

An experimental interface created with DeepSeek's DeepSite to enhance human creativity. This prototype served as the foundation for developing the more advanced Problem_Solver tool, demonstrating how interfaces can be designed to augment creative problem-solving capabilities.

Problem_Solver

Building upon the SmartSolver interface concept, Problem_Solver is a superproductivity tool that leverages advanced artificial intelligence models to generate new perspectives on complex problems. By applying innovative prompt engineering techniques, this software facilitates the discovery of creative and effective solutions quickly and efficiently. It represents a concrete example of how technology can amplify our creative and problem-solving potential.

Who I Am

My name is Gavino Giovanni Marras, a visionary manager with expertise in future thinking and human-centered innovation as co-founder of Lato Innovation Design, a strategic consulting boutique specialized in guiding organizations toward their desired future.

I explore how emerging technologies are amplifying human productivity, creating unprecedented societal transformations. This site serves as a concrete demonstration of the SuperProductivity concept through practical applications, articles, books, and software.
